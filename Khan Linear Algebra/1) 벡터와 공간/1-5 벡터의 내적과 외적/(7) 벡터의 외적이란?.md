- 내적(Dot Product)
	(1) $\vec{a}, \vec{b} \in R^n$ => **scalar**
- 외적(Cross Product)
	- only defined in $R^3$ => **vector**

- $\vec{a}$ = $[a_1, a_2, a_3]$, $\vec{b}$ = $[b_1, b_2, b_3]$
	- $\vec{a}\times\vec{b}$ = $[a_2b_3 - a_3b_2, a_3b_1 - a_1b_3, a_1b_2 - a_2 b_1]$
